Cross-domain Few-shot Learning Project

Introduction

This is a research project.

  • We proposed a new and realistic setting, cross-domain few-shot learning and designed a benchmark for this setting.
  • We proposed a contrastive learning based approach and a rotation based approach and our methods improve the accuracy from 60% (the baseline) to 70%.
  • We published two papers based on the above results.

Dataset Overivew

We built our dataset on top of DomainNet, which has 6 domains for each categories.
